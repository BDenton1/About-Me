My first personal music device was an iPod nano that my dad gave me for Christmas. He was able to brainwash
me with his musical taste this way. These days, I use various apps on my phone. My preferred source is Sound Cloud.
Sound Cloud has been responsible for the popularization of countless underground artists over the years, and
